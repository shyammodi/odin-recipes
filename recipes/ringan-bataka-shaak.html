Ringan Bataka Shaak

Description

Ringan Bataka Shaak, also known as Baingan Aloo Sabzi, is a flavorful and spiced Indian curry made with eggplant (ringan) and potatoes (bataka). This vegetarian dish is a delightful medley of tender eggplant and potatoes cooked in a well-balanced blend of spices. To prepare this dish, eggplant and potatoes are cut into bite-sized pieces and sautéed with mustard seeds, cumin, and other spices until they are coated with the aromatic mixture. The addition of tomatoes, ginger, and garlic enhances the savory flavors, creating a delicious and satisfying curry. Ringan Bataka Shaak is often served with warm roti or steamed rice, making it a perfect choice for a wholesome and homely meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant (aubergine), cut into cubes
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Allow them to splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add diced eggplant and potatoes. Stir well to coat them with the spices.
  5. Cover and cook until the vegetables are tender. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander.
  6. Serve hot with roti or rice. Enjoy the delightful flavors of Ringan Bataka Shaak!
