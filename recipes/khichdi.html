Khichdi

Description

Khichdi, a wholesome and comforting dish, is a classic Indian one-pot meal made with a combination of rice and lentils. This simple and nutritious dish is often considered easy on the stomach and is a go-to comfort food. To prepare Khichdi, rice and lentils are cooked together with aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and turmeric until they form a soft and porridge-like consistency. The dish can be customized with additional vegetables, and it is commonly served with a dollop of ghee or yogurt for added richness. Khichdi is not only delicious but also a nourishing option for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup split yellow moong dal (lentils)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice and lentils together and soak for 30 minutes.
  2. In a pressure cooker, heat ghee and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  4. Drain the soaked rice and lentils and add them to the pressure cooker. Add water and pressure cook until well-cooked.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot. Enjoy this wholesome Khichdi on its own or with yogurt.
